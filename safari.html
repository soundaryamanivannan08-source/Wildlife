Best Time to Visit

Timing your safari is crucial for the best wildlife viewing experience. The African savanna changes dramatically with the seasons.

June to October: Dry season. Animals congregate around waterholes. Clear skies, cooling temperatures, and prime conditions for the Great Migration crossings.
November to May: Green season. Lush green landscapes, bird migrations, and newborn calving in the Serengeti. Ideal for photography with fewer crowds.
April to May: Heavy rain period. Concessions become lush and green, offering dramatic photography backdrops, though some low-lying mobile camps close.
